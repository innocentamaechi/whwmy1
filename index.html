The truth most relationship advice avoids

Men do not "arrive" at commitment over time.

They lean toward it — or away from it — early. And that direction is shaped by three specific things.

How a woman makes him feel about himself
Not about how much she loves him. About what her presence does to his sense of self.
02
How he sees his future with her
Whether she fits into the life he is building — or feels separate from it.

The role the relationship plays in his life
